# FAQ

## Account

### How long after registration can I use the platform?

An administrator will review your account within 1 business day. You'll receive an email notification once approved.

### What if I forget my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and the system will send a reset link.

## Templates

### How long does template review take?

Usually 1-3 business days. You'll be notified of the result via in-app notification and email.

### What if my template is rejected?

The review result includes an explanation. You can modify and resubmit. Common rejection reasons:
- Insufficient prompt quality
- Content doesn't meet platform guidelines
- Missing effective showcase images

### Can I keep my templates private?

Yes. Templates default to PENDING status (draft) after creation. Without submitting for review, they remain private and only usable by you.

## Generation

### How many credits does generating an image cost?

Depends on generation mode and resolution. Standard text-to-image costs 5 credits/image, HD costs 10 credits/image. See [Credits & Plans](pricing) for details.

### Can generated images be used commercially?

Yes, images generated with Basic plan and above can be used commercially. The platform provides copyright documentation.

### What if generation is slow?

- Check your internet connection
- Pro plan has priority queue for faster response
- Brief queuing may occur during peak hours

## Credits

### Do credits expire?

- Subscription credits: Valid for current month, no rollover
- Top-up credits: Never expire
- Reward credits: Valid for 90 days

### Can I get a refund?

Consumed credits are non-refundable. Unused top-up credits can be refunded within 7 days.

## Technical

### Which browsers are supported?

Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 15+, Edge 90+. Latest Chrome recommended.

### How is data security ensured?

- All communications use HTTPS encryption
- Works are private by default, not publicly accessible
- User works are not used for model training
- Enterprise edition supports private deployment
